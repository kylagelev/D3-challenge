Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

A Few Insights:

With a lower median income, there are greater obesity rates. Greater percent of smokers in the majority of states fall into Generation X (remember, Census data is from 2010). Lower median income, greater lack in healthcare. States with greater poverty rates, have lower rates of lack in healthcare. Might be indicative of Medicaid. Though, lower poverty rates correlate also with lack of healthcare.
